Eflyn.com Website Design

About Project

Building the online face of Eflyn, a frontrunner in the design and manufacture of next-generation digital display, self checkout kiosks, and automation technologies was a mission-critical project. My role was not only to design but also to develop an end-to-end website that effectively showcases Eflyn's innovative product offerings and supports essential business functions such as marketing, branding, customer acquisition, and product knowledge presentation.


"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works." - Steve Jobs

This project involved:

  • Creating a fully custom-built Bootstrap 3 theme with hundreds of bespoke components tailored for a wide range of needs.
  • Integrating various content management systems and APIs for a seamless user experience.
  • Designing and developing a custom kiosk preview generator that automatically maps sample media to hundreds of kiosk models, allowing customers to visualize potential kiosk solutions effortlessly.
  • Putting in place thorough testing and development mechanisms to ensure optimal site speed and performance.

It was not only about delivering a website; it was about crafting a digital identity that resonates with Eflyn's commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction. Reflecting back, I am proud of what we accomplished.
