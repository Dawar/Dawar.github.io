Unified Payments for Kiosks

About Project

Ensuring security and efficiency in payment processing for kiosks is paramount. My objective was to develop a Unified Payment Solution to interface with multiple payment processors, providing a reliable and secure method of processing payments in Eflyn kiosks.

This project involved:

  • Writing the initial payment hardware integrations for Eflyn kiosks and apps.
  • Developing a Unified Payment Solution that encapsulates existing and future payment integrations.
  • Designing a generic interface for the payment system to be used by third-party developers through easy-to-use SDKs and APIs.
  • Integrating a wide variety of payment processing partners including Global Payments, Clover, TSYS / Cayan, TD Merchant Solutions, Moneris, Stripe, and TouchNet OneCard.

This system not only enhanced the security of payment processing in Eflyn kiosks but also significantly streamlined the payment integration process. I am proud to have played a central role in developing this critical aspect of Eflyn's service offering.
