The Kiosk Developer Platform

About Project

Building on Eflyn's decade-long experience with kiosk apps, I developed the Kiosk Developer Platform. This tool allows developers to create custom kiosk apps, leveraging our established platform and proven technologies.


"The ones who are crazy enough to think they can change the world, are the ones that do." - Steve Jobs

This project involved:

  • Building a fully secured Kiosk Desktop runtime for powerful custom apps.
  • Providing easy access to Kiosk APIs and SDKs, enabling developers to use kiosk-specific functions, such as printing receipts, processing payments, scanning barcodes, and more.
  • Making Eflyn's expertise and extensive documentation available to developers.
  • Facilitating integration of custom kiosk apps with Eflyn Kiosk apps for enhanced functionalities.
  • Enabling automatic analytics for kiosk apps and remote management of kiosk machines.
  • Offering accessibility mode and control over app deployment.
  • Building multiple Node.JS microservices and developing fully typed APIs and SDKs in Typescript.

Creating the Kiosk Developer Platform from scratch was a challenging yet rewarding process. It involved understanding the needs of developers, conceptualizing solutions, and finally implementing these solutions in a way that makes the task of developing custom kiosk apps easier, faster, and more efficient. Reflecting back, I am proud of this accomplishment.
