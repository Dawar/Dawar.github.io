Hardware Product Design: 32" Self Order Kiosk

About Project

One of my significant involvements with Eflyn was the development of the flagship product, the 32" Self Order Kiosk. This project demanded a deep dive into product design, requiring involvement in 3D rendering, material selection, hardware components, and electrical safety.


"Design is a balance between form and function... it takes two." - Seesaw

This project involved:

  • Creating realistic 3D renderings to visualize the final product and its fit in potential use scenarios.
  • Choosing materials with the perfect blend of durability, aesthetics, and cost-effectiveness.
  • Selecting hardware components that would ensure seamless functionality, reliability, and longevity of the kiosk.
  • Ensuring the electrical safety of the product, making it not only efficient but also safe for use in various environments.

Contributing to the design and development of Eflyn's flagship product was a challenging but fulfilling experience. It was an opportunity to delve into the complexities of hardware product design and witness the product come to life from a mere idea. Reflecting back, I am proud of our accomplishment and its continued success in the market.
